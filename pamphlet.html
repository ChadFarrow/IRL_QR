How to Pay with CashApp

Follow these steps to pay a Lightning invoice using your CashApp cash balance.

1

Open CashApp & Click the $ Icon

Open CashApp on your mobile device. You'll see the main screen with a keypad showing "$0".

2

Scan the QR Code

Tap the scan QR button in the top left corner to scan the QR code displayed on the payment page.

3

Select Cash as Funding Source

CashApp will ask how you want to fund your bitcoin payment. Select Cash. You may also see Bitcoin listed if you have any.

4

Review & Confirm Payment

You'll see a confirmation screen showing the payment amount with all the details. Tap to confirm.

5

Payment Successful!

After confirming, you'll see a success screen with a green checkmark confirming your payment was processed.

6

View Your Payment Receipt

View the payment receipt in your transaction history. Tap the clock icon in the bottom navigation for your Activity feed.

7

View Transaction Details

Tap on the transaction in your Activity feed to see detailed payment information.

Scan the QR code at so-big-lightning-payment.vercel.app to get started!
